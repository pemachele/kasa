About KASA

Inspire Tanzanians to explore study abroad opportunities

KASA ltd was conceived with a simple friendly question over the phone - "Can you help find a good oversea university for my child? "

As a relatively graduate from one of the top ranked university in India, Kasole Simon received a number of requests from families in Tanzania to help place their kids in Oversee universities. What started out as a favour to friends and family soon took up practically all of his time. However, this development did not bother him in the least, because he felt a sense of satisfaction every time an application was successful and he received an admission letter. It was only a matter of time before he turned this new-found hobby into a full time business.
